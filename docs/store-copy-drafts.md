## Positioning Rule

Describe Frostwake as an original Windows multiplayer survival deduction game about keeping a near-future icebound research vessel alive under sabotage pressure. Do not market it by naming or implying another game's brand.

## Long Description Skeleton

### English

Frostwake is a crew-based survival deduction game set aboard a near-future research vessel trapped on a hostile polar route.

The crew must keep the vessel moving by repairing damaged systems, managing heat and flooding, recovering supplies, and restoring route and radio equipment. Every task asks players to cooperate, split up, report what they saw, and decide who can be trusted.

One or two agents are working against the crew. They can sabotage critical systems, hide supplies, fake repairs, isolate players, and turn ordinary accidents into convincing cover stories.

Built around short, tense matches, Frostwake focuses on readable ship systems and post-round arguments that start because of what players actually did in the match.

### Japanese

Frostwakeは、氷海の航路上で孤立した近未来研究船を舞台にした、乗員制の協力サバイバル推理ゲームです。

乗員は、船体、発電、燃料、浸水、暖房、無線、航路装置を維持しながら船を進めます。修理、物資回収、船外作業は協力を必要としますが、誰が何をしたかを常に確認できるとは限りません。

乗員の中には、任務達成を妨害する工作員が紛れています。工作員は重要設備を壊し、物資を隠し、修理を偽装し、孤立した乗員を事故に見せかけて排除しようとします。

Frostwakeは短く濃い試合、読み取りやすい船内システム、そして試合後に自然発生する議論を中心に設計します。
