## Header

- Cycle: 99

- Rust `summarize_lobby_metadata`: surface `mode`/`difficulty` in the `[PASS]` output.
- Tests: accept madman/hard; reject unknown mode/difficulty (schema enum, enforced by `validate_schema_value`).
