## Why a canvas now (not throwaway)
Megascans **surfaces are materials applied to your own geometry**; **3D ice/rock are meshes you
scatter**. So a large ground plane is the permanent foundation — only its material changes when the
Megascans snow surface arrives. Building it now lets the owner download assets (login-gated) in
parallel while the loop has the field ready to dress.
