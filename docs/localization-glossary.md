## Tone Rules

- English: direct and functional; avoid parody, clone, or "for fans of X" phrasing.
- Japanese: prefer concrete system nouns over emotional marketing; keep horror wording restrained.
- Simplified Chinese: keep technical terms stable and avoid implying official relation to any existing game.

## Forbidden Or Reserved Terms

Do not use these in public copy, UI, file names for final assets, or Steam metadata:

- Dread
- Hunger
- Thrall
- spiritual successor
- refugee
- unofficial sequel
- occult
- ritual
- cannibalism
- human meat
- poison stew
