# GP-08: Distinct Presentation And Rights

## North-Star Goal

Frostwake looks, sounds, and sells as a distinct, legally safe near-future icebound research-vessel game, with every shippable asset and public claim traceable to approved sources.

## Why This Exists

Presentation drives commercial trust, but premature art can hide a weak game and create IP risk. This portfolio continuously improves identity, asset provenance, and store readiness in lockstep with playability evidence.

## Health Signals

| Signal | Healthy Direction |
| --- | --- |
| Distinct identity | Names, visuals, audio, UI, lore, and copy avoid protected expression and confusing comparisons |
| Asset provenance | Every candidate has source, license, commercial-use, redistribution, attribution, and approval state |
| Visual relevance | Screenshots and POC scenes show actual product state, not misleading concepts |
| Art timing | Production art starts after human proof clarifies what matters |
| AI disclosure | AI-assisted content is recorded and safe for intended use |
| Store claim safety | Public copy claims only implemented or clearly scheduled features |

## Improvement Questions

- Which visual or audio choice most strengthens Frostwake's own identity?
- Which asset candidate has the highest value with the lowest rights risk?
- Which public claim could mislead players or reviewers?
- Which placeholder is becoming risky because players may confuse it with final direction?
- What evidence is needed before committing production art spend?

## Evidence Standard

Use asset ledger entries, scorecards, IP review notes, visual POC screenshots, store-copy reviews, and AI disclosure updates. Public-facing material should be checked against actual implementation.

## Boundaries

- Do not import assets without provenance and approval state.
- Do not alter the whitebox automation map for art experiments.
- Do not use protected names, roles, lore, UI composition, map layouts, or marketing claims from comparable games.
- Do not present concept-only material as gameplay screenshots.

## Review Cadence

Review before asset import, before screenshots, before store updates, and after playability decisions change the vertical-slice scope.

## Agent Charter

An agent assigned to this portfolio should improve identity or rights confidence. A good assignment is: "Review the top asset candidates against the IP boundary and identify which visual POC direction is safest to explore next."
